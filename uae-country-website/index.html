United Arab Emirates

About The U.A.E

The United Arab Emirates (UAE), is a country in West Asia, in the Middle East. It is a federal, elective monarchy composed of seven emirates, with Abu Dhabi as its capital.

The 7 Emirates

Here is the list of the 7 different emirates (states) that were founded during the formation and establishment of the United Arab Emirates:
